The Story Behind PhyChat

Revolutionizing physics education through AI-powered personalized learning

Our Mission

PhyChat was born from a simple observation: many students struggle with physics not because the subject is inherently difficult, but because they lack access to personalized, patient, and knowledgeable tutoring.

Our mission is to democratize physics education by providing an AI-powered learning assistant that's available 24/7, understands your questions naturally, and adapts to your learning pace and style.

Our Approach

RAG + LLM: The Perfect Combination

Traditional chatbots often suffer from "hallucinations" - generating plausible-sounding but incorrect information. PhyChat solves this by combining Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) with Large Language Models (LLM).

Knowledge Retrieval

Before generating any response, our system retrieves relevant information from a curated database of physics knowledge, ensuring accuracy and reliability.

Intelligent Generation

The LLM then synthesizes this retrieved information into a natural, coherent response that's easy to understand and educationally valuable.

Quality Assurance

Every response is grounded in verified physics knowledge, minimizing errors and ensuring students receive accurate information.
